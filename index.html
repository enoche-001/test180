FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need before placing your order. If you don't find your answer here, our WhatsApp line is always open.

💬 Chat on WhatsApp
What products does Emmanice Essentials offer? +
We offer handcrafted shoes, slides, belts and bags for men and women. Custom sizes and colours are available on request.
How do I place a custom footwear order? +
Browse available items in the shop, or contact us on WhatsApp for custom sizes and colours. Share your measurements and style preference — we'll handle the rest.
Do you make shoes for events or groups? +
Yes. We handle group orders for weddings, events and cooperate teams. The more you order, the better the price. Reach out on WhatsApp for a group quote.
How long does it take to make my shoes? +
Standard orders are ready within 3–7 business days. Custom or bulk orders may take slightly longer. We always confirm your expected date when you order.
Can I track my order? +
Yes — use your order ID on our order tracking page. You'll also get WhatsApp updates throughout the process.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers and mobile payment. Payment details are shared at checkout or during your WhatsApp conversation. We confirm payment before production begins.
Do you deliver outside Lokoja? +
Yes, we deliver across Nigeria. Delivery fees vary by location. You can also pick up at our shop opposite Muslim Community Secondary School, Lokoja. We use reliable logistics to get your order safely.
